Who We Are

OrozcoRealty Is a Real Estate consumer platform design to enhance buyer knowledge by providing a systematic approach to home buying.
By using Artificial Intelligence as the Engine behind Process, we provide clear and unbiased information and statistics.
We hope to breach the knowledge gap between You and Realtors in order to effectively achieve your home buying goals.

Our HIstory: 

Founded in Mexico in 1970s by “Chabella” Rivera de Orozco. Orozco Realty began with a simple but POWERFUL mission: To provide housing accommodations to anyone, regardless of financial circumstances.  Driven by compassion and a deep sense of community Chabella sought to ensure all had access to a place to call home.

Our Present

OrozcoRealty is a veteran-owned, family-based business with a mission to Empower the Military Community. We are an A.I Powered Real Estate Intelligence Company designed to enhance the buying process through the use of Psychology, Data Science, and A.I Assisted Technology.

Our Future

Over the decades that founding philosophy remains at the heart of OrozcoRealty.  Now with over 20 years of managerial experience and Master’s degree from both Duke, and Johns Hopkins University, the company is perfectly aligned to Empower both Buyers and Realtors.

We are the Future of Real Estate!
